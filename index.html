Our Story

Mama Greens is the brainchild of five visionary developers

Who upon noticed the decline in sales decided in Mama Mboga Business

and looked into the state of Mama Mboga business in Nairobi.

Finding no end to end tool on the market to link vegetable vendors

with their customers, they set up to develop one that actively addressed MM's pain point.

They also realized that despite digitalization of many business sectors,

local fruits and vegetable vendors were excluded from this migration

Mama Greens has revolutionized the local fresh fruits and vegetables

sector such that it is easier for Mama Mboga to market her products and reach her customers

via digital strategies.
